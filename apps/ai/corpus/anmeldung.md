Within 14 days of moving into a home in Germany you must register your address at the local Buergeramt (registration office). You need your passport or ID, a landlord confirmation (Wohnungsgeberbestaetigung) and usually your rental contract. After registering you receive a registration certificate (Meldebescheinigung) that is required to open a bank account, receive your tax ID and many other steps. Book an appointment in advance because slots fill quickly.

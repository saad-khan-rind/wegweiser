title: The asylum procedure
origin: bamf
updated_at: 2026-06-22
tags: topic:asylum, status:asylum
---
People seeking protection first report as asylum seekers and then lodge a formal application at a branch of the Federal Office for Migration and Refugees (BAMF). You receive an arrival certificate (Ankunftsnachweis) and later an invitation to a personal interview, which is the most important step. You have the right to an interpreter, and independent counseling services can help you prepare.

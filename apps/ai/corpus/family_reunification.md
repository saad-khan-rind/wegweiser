Family reunification allows close family members to join relatives in Germany when the legal requirements are met. The required proof depends on the family relationship and the status of the person in Germany. Typical documents include a valid passport, biometric photo, marriage certificate or birth certificate, proof of the family member's residence status in Germany, proof of housing or livelihood when required, and health insurance proof.

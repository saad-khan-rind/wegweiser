Health insurance is mandatory for everyone living in Germany. Most newcomers join a public health insurer (gesetzliche Krankenkasse). After you choose an insurer and submit the membership form, you receive an insurance card that you present at every doctor and pharmacy. Public insurance is the simplest starting point for most people.

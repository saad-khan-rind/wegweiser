The Opportunity Card can support a job search in Germany when the person meets the legal criteria. The route depends on qualification, points or recognised credentials, livelihood, and health insurance. Typical documents include a valid passport, biometric photo, qualification documents, proof of livelihood, health insurance proof, and evidence for the points or recognition criteria.

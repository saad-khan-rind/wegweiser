A residence permit (Aufenthaltstitel) determines how long you may stay in Germany and whether you may work or study. Permits must be renewed before they expire at the Auslaenderbehoerde. Typical documents include a valid passport, a biometric photo, proof of health insurance and proof of income or enrolment. Appointment slots are limited, so book your renewal well in advance.

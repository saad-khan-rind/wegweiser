Residence routes for skilled work depend on the job, recognised qualifications, salary, and whether the employment is qualified. The EU Blue Card is one route for qualified employment when the legal requirements are met. Typical documents include a valid passport, biometric photo, employment contract or concrete job offer, proof of qualification or recognition, proof of health insurance, and sometimes salary evidence. The Auslaenderbehoerde or German mission checks the exact route and work permission.

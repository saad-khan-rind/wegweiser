People who want to study in Germany usually need admission or conditional admission from a recognised higher education institution, proof that their livelihood is secured, and health insurance. If they are abroad and need a stay longer than 90 days, they normally apply for a national visa before entering Germany and later receive a residence permit for studies from the Auslaenderbehoerde. Typical documents include a valid passport, biometric photos, admission or enrolment proof, proof of financing or secure livelihood, and health insurance proof.

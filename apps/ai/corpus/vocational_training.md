People planning recognised vocational training in Germany may need a national visa or residence permit for training. The application normally depends on a training contract or training place, secure livelihood, health insurance, and identity documents. Typical documents include a valid passport, biometric photo, training contract, school or qualification documents, proof of livelihood or financing, and health insurance proof.

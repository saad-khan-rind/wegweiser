Whether you are allowed to work in Germany depends on your residence status and how long you have been in the country. Permission to work is recorded in your residence document by the Auslaenderbehoerde. Asylum seekers may be allowed to work after a waiting period; recognised refugees and many permit holders may work without restriction. Rules have changed recently, so always check the current date on the official source or ask a counselor if your case is unclear.
